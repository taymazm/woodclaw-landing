How it works

Configure once. Run on any target. Track the pipeline. WoodClaw reads structured Markdown files describing your company, products, qualification rubric, outreach template, and funnel — then applies them to every research run.

1

Configure your vertical

Fill in five Markdown files: company positioning, products you buy or sell, qualification rubric, outreach template, funnel stages. Or use a built-in preset (sawn timber marketplace, contract furniture manufacturer).

2

Drop in a target

Paste a company name. WoodClaw runs web research, applies hard filters, scores on a 100-point rubric, assigns a Tier 1–5, picks the right outreach variant, and writes the brief + drafted email.

3

Track in the pipeline

Every run saves to a Kanban-style pipeline with 11 funnel statuses. Move cards, export to CSV, batch-run 10 targets at once. The brief and the outreach are right there — you just review and send.

Built for the vertical, not bolted on.

Every feature exists because someone running supplier acquisition in this industry needs it.

🔍

Vertical-aware web research

Searches for Fordaq profiles, FSC/PEFC certifications, EUDR readiness, kiln-drying capacity, named contacts. Cites sources by URL. Marks unknowns as "unknown" — never invents facts.

📊

Configurable qualification rubric

Hard filters first (wrong species → parked), then 100-point scoring (capacity, certifications, recent activity, accessibility), then Tier 1–5 assignment. Your rubric, your weights.

✉️

Multi-variant outreach

Five outreach variants per target: standard, sophisticated-target, small-mill, hot-lead (specific dimension match), certified-buyer, DE/AT (German B1). Picks the right one based on what research turns up.

📋

Kanban pipeline + CSV export

11-status funnel collapsed into 5 Kanban columns (Lead, Engaged, Quoting, Won, Lost/Parked). Move cards by changing status. Export the full pipeline to CSV one click.

📦

Batch runs

Paste 10 names. The agent works through them sequentially, saves each brief, shows tier + summary at the end. End-of-week scan in one job.

⚙️

Adapts to your company

Built-in presets for sawn timber marketplaces and contract furniture manufacturers. Swap five Markdown files to fit a panel supplier, a veneer importer, a hardware distributor — anything in wood B2B.
